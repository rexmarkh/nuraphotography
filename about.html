About

NURA Photography, owned and operated by G. Arunkumar, offers Creative Candid 

Photojournalism with a modern feel.

Perfecting the art of wedding and event photography for more than 7 years, Nura blends

technical proficiency with aesthetic intuition to produce off-beat, artistic and contemporary

photographs.

In Nura, we believe Photography is about capturing souls and not just smiles. With the

characteristic variety of mood and tone so evident in our work, Nura's portfolios organically

combine the sensitivity and vulnerability of our clients with our ability to bring out their often

uninhibited and gregarious personalities. Be it an exquisite pre-wedding shoot or a luring luxury

wedding shoot, our photographs and wedding films will talk volumes.

Wedding is an amalgamation of tradition, culture and rich emotions filled with subtle and

overwhelming moments. Capturing peoples’ moods and expressions is an intriguing subject for

Wedding Photographers. We ensure every nuance of your wedding is brilliantly captured

making it your treasured memories for a lifetime; be it the colorful décor, the bridal makeup and

trousseau, the funny pranks, the family drama, the crazy friends, the romantic moments, the tears  of joy and what not?? 

Nura’s broad capabilities and diverse range of style has allowed us to shoot a wide array of

weddings, ranging from big destination weddings to small intimate city weddings capturing the

unique spirit of each wedding. For us, every wedding is special and every couple is our favorite.

We at Nura aspire to create a magical wedding for everyone who believes in us. We make your

dream wedding come true by capturing the forever moments eternalized in a frame. Our well

shot pictures will narrate your fairy tale wedding for years to come and make you relive your

memories every time you look at them.

Wedding Photography is our Passion... It is our Job... It is our Calling… AND WE SIMPLY

LOVE IT!!!
